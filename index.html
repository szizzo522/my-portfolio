About Me

Results-driven Computer Science student specializing in cybersecurity, threat detection, and secure systems engineering, with industry-recognized certifications including CompTIA Security+, CySA+, Network+, A+, along with IBM and Cisco credentials.

I bring a blend of technical depth and real-world operational experience. I have designed, built, and tested secure environments, encrypted applications, and cyber-range labs to simulate real attack and defense scenarios using tools such as Kali Linux, Metasploit, Nmap, and AWS IAM, with a focus on secure access controls and incident response workflows. My hands-on projects include a cryptographically protected password vault, a real-time malware and file-risk scanner, and a home security lab used to practice detection, exploitation, and remediation techniques.

These experiences strengthened my ability to troubleshoot under pressure, document incidents, and communicate technical issues clearly—core skills in SOC and incident response environments.

Beyond technology, I am an Eagle Scout, having led and executed a community leadership project that raised over $1,500, coordinated volunteers, and delivered custom-built equipment to support a shelter-to-service dog program. This experience reinforced my leadership, accountability, and project execution skills—qualities I bring into every technical role.

I combine cybersecurity expertise, business awareness, and proven leadership to help organizations protect sensitive data, maintain compliance, and remain resilient during security incidents. I am actively seeking opportunities in cybersecurity, healthcare technology security, and threat analysis where I can contribute from day one.
